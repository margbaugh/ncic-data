Resources

 

I want resources on a topic

Each year, NIC creates resource pages to address key topics in the corrections field. These topic pages are concentrated collections of content around the subject area, both from NIC's own research as well as other expert sources across the internet.

 

I want resources based on my role

When you want to find resources that are directly related to your area of practice, your role, the pages in this section have been created for you.

  • I want the latest NIC News

    NIC's News and Events page details our latest press releases, corrections news, and any events we host.

  • Which Conferences will NIC attend?

    Each year, NIC's staff attend a number of conferences across the United States. To find out which ones we regularly attend or to invite us to your conference, please explore this page.
