Topics in Corrections

What is a Topic page?
Topic pages each contain a collection of the best NIC publications, videos, and media as well as news articles and resources from the field on a given corrections subject. They are designed as a starting point to learn about a topic and take an initial dive into the subject matter. After reading and watching the resources, you should have moderate to expert knowledge about the topic and know what to search for next if you need to continue learning about the subject.
